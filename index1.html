Summer Villa Emilia

Summer Villa Emilia is a perfect place for luxury rest, traveling and tasting the best regional cuisine in Italy. The property features several queen beds, a full bed and a sofa. Private parking is also available.

Summer Villa Emilia is a perfect place for luxury rest, traveling and tasting the best regional cuisine in Italy. The property features several queen beds, a full bed and a sofa. Private parking is also available.

The bathroom is equipped with a hot tube and lots of spa services, so you may feel yourself in a spa salon.

The region where you go on tour includes Renaissance cities like Bologna, Ferrara, and Modena, beautiful stretches of coastline some of the top best restaurants.
